2. Mobile App Development

  • Mental Health Support App: Develop a mobile application that provides accessible and anonymous mental health resources and support tools.

3. Artificial Intelligence 

  • Smart Traffic Management System in Aviation: Design an AI-powered traffic management system that optimizes air traffic flow, reduces congestion, and improves overall commute times.
  •  Streamlining Complex Code: Design an AI-powered tool that Analyzes code & identifies complexity.

  • Detecting Counterfeit Cash:  Build an app that

Rules & GUIDELINES

  • BYOS (Bring Your Own System): Ensures that each team has the necessary tools to participate effectively, maintaining a level playing field.
  • Designated Color for Identification: Facilitates easy identification of teams and fosters a sense of unity and identity among team members.
  • Team Requirement: Team size must comprise of 4 members. To encourage Female participation ,Teams with at least one female member will only be entertained. 
  • Diversity and Inclusivity: Encourages diverse perspectives and representation within teams, promoting a more inclusive environment in tech.
  • Attendance Requirement: Ensures active participation and collaboration throughout the event, preventing teams from being understaffed or inactive.
  • Ethical Conduct: Upholds the integrity of the hackathon by discouraging unethical behaviors such as cheating or plagiarism, fostering a professional and respectful atmosphere.
